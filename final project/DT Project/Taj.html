Hyderabadi Biryani

Price: ₹900

A fragrant basmati rice dish cooked with tender marinated chicken, saffron, and a secret blend of spices, served with raita.

Ingredients:

  • Basmati rice
  • Marinated chicken
  • Saffron
  • Spices (cinnamon, cardamom, cloves)
  • Yogurt

Recipe:

Layer cooked rice and marinated chicken, add saffron milk, and slow-cook to perfection. Serve hot with raita.

Butter Chicken

Price: ₹700

A creamy tomato-based curry cooked with succulent chicken pieces, butter, and aromatic spices, served with naan or rice.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Tomatoes
  • Butter
  • Cream
  • Spices (garam masala, chili powder, fenugreek)

Recipe:

Cook chicken in a rich tomato gravy, add cream and butter, and simmer until flavorful. Serve with naan or steamed rice.
