Signature Dishes

Traditional Hyderabadi Biryani

Price: ₹1000

A fragrant blend of basmati rice, tender meat, and spices slow-cooked to perfection. Served with a side of cooling raita and a boiled egg.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Chicken/Beef
  • Yogurt
  • Spices (Saffron, Cinnamon, Cardamom)
  • Boiled egg

Recipe:

Marinate the meat with yogurt and spices, layer with partially cooked rice, and steam cook for 45 minutes to blend the flavors.

Royal Indian Thali

Price: ₹850

A grand spread of various traditional Indian curries, breads, rice, and sides, served on a single platter for a complete meal.

Ingredients:

  • Dal (lentils)
  • Paneer (cottage cheese)
  • Seasonal vegetables
  • Roti (Indian bread)
  • Rice

Recipe:

Prepare various curries and sides, serve with freshly baked roti, and offer a mix of spicy and mild dishes for a balanced experience.
