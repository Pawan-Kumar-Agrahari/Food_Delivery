Featured Dishes

Margherita Pizza

Price: ₹650

A classic Margherita pizza with a crispy thin crust, topped with fresh mozzarella, tangy tomato sauce, and fragrant basil leaves.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella
  • Basil leaves
  • Olive oil

Recipe:

Roll out the pizza dough, spread tomato sauce, layer with fresh mozzarella, and bake until crispy. Top with fresh basil leaves and drizzle with olive oil.

Classic Chocolate Soufflé

Price: ₹500

A rich and decadent chocolate soufflé with a warm molten center, served with a dusting of powdered sugar and a side of vanilla ice cream.

Ingredients:

  • Dark chocolate
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla ice cream

Recipe:

Melt the dark chocolate and mix with eggs, sugar, and butter. Bake the soufflé until set on the outside but still molten in the center. Serve with vanilla ice cream.
